An observability setup focused on useful production visibility rather than dashboards for their own sake.

The stack combines metrics, logs, alert routing, and operational habits that help engineers understand what changed and where to look first.

## Engineering Focus

- Prometheus metrics and service-level dashboards.
- Grafana views for operational triage.
- Loki logs for incident context.
- Alertmanager routing with actionable alerts.
